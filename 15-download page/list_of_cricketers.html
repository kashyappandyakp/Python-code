Captains[edit]

Main article: Indian national cricket captains

A total of 33 players have captained the Indian Test team.[9] Of those who have captained more than two Test matches, Virat Kohli has the highest win rate (57.77%). Mahendra Singh Dhoni has captained the team for the most number of Test matches (60). As of 2018, Nawab of Pataudi, Jr. remains the youngest captain for India in test matches.[10]
